Careers at KCD

KCD hires and trains crews the same way it wins contracts — for the long haul. Build a career on the projects that build the region.

Why KCD

Real work on the region's most significant projects

KCD crews work on major infrastructure — highway corridors, bridges, transit projects, and active airport environments at SEA-TAC. That means steady, meaningful work with experienced operators, and the chance to build skills that carry a career.

🚛

Operators & Drivers

CDL drivers and sweeper, water truck, and plow operators for scheduled and on-call work across King, Pierce, and Snohomish Counties.

🦺

Field & Site Crews

Site support, cleanup, and facility-service roles supporting active construction projects and project offices.

📈

Room to Grow

A growing company means growing responsibility — crews are trained up, not just hired on.

A Career, Not Just a Job

Built on second chances that become real careers

KCD's founder knows firsthand what it takes to build a second chance into a real career — and that understanding shapes how KCD hires, trains, and promotes.

Not ready yet? Pay It 4ward, the nonprofit founded by KCD's owner, provides CDL training and trade-based education in Seattle's Central District — a direct path toward the credentials this work requires.
