What KCD Does Today

Core Services

The sweeping, trucking, water, hauling, and winter-response services that help keep construction projects, roadways, jobsites, and transportation infrastructure moving.

Cornerstone Service

Street & Construction Sweeping

Street sweeping is a cornerstone of KCD Trucking. We support active construction projects, roadways, transportation corridors, and complex work environments where cleanliness, visibility, safety, and schedule coordination matter.

  • Construction, highway & roadway sweeping
  • Nighttime sweeping operations
  • Jobsite and access-road sweeping
  • Track-out and debris removal
  • FOD-focused sweeping and debris-control support in applicable project environments
  • Scheduled and responsive sweeping support

Moved Safely, On Schedule

Dump Trucking & Hauling

KCD provides dependable trucking and hauling support for construction and infrastructure projects that need materials moved safely, efficiently, and on schedule.

  • Construction material hauling
  • Dirt and aggregate transportation
  • Debris and material removal
  • Heavy-civil project trucking support
  • Solo dump and side-dump support, based on project requirements and fleet availability
  • Scheduled and on-demand hauling support

Dust Control & Site Support

Water Truck Services

Our water truck services support active construction operations where dust suppression, site conditions, and project requirements demand dependable water application.

  • Dust suppression
  • Construction-site watering
  • Roadway and haul-route dust control
  • Heavy-civil construction support
  • Project-specific water truck operations

Winter Response

Snow & Ice Response

KCD provides winter-response support for clients that need dependable service when snow and ice affect access, safety, and operations.

  • Snow plowing
  • Deicing support
  • Commercial and construction-site response
  • Emergency winter operations
