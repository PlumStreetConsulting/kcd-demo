About KCD Trucking

Show up prepared. Work safely. Perform.

KCD Trucking is an experienced infrastructure and construction support contractor built around one simple commitment: show up prepared, work safely, and perform the job the project requires.

Who We Are

Straightforward, essential, and built on real field experience

Our core business is straightforward and essential. We provide the sweeping, trucking, water, hauling, and winter-response services that help keep construction projects, roadways, jobsites, and transportation infrastructure moving.

KCD has grown through real field experience on major public and private projects. We understand demanding schedules, night work, changing site conditions, restricted-access environments, and the importance of being a subcontractor that project teams can rely on.

🎯

Our Mission

Dependable service. Safe operations. Proven performance.

Our mission is to provide reliable trucking and construction-support services that help contractors, agencies, and project teams keep work moving safely and efficiently. We are committed to building long-term relationships through communication, accountability, responsiveness, and consistent performance in the field.

🧭

Our Vision

Our vision is to continue building KCD into one of the most trusted infrastructure-support contractors in the markets we serve — known for strong operations, reliable equipment, experienced people, and the ability to perform on increasingly complex projects.

We are focused on growing from a proven specialty subcontractor into a stronger infrastructure business while staying grounded in the services and performance that built the company.

Leadership — Built From the Truck Up

Kelly Jefferson — Founder & CEO

Kelly Jefferson founded KCD Trucking in 2017 on a street-sweeping and dump-trucking base, and has built it into a company with direct relationships across leading general contractors and the Port of Seattle.

That growth is rooted in the same background Kelly brings to workforce development across South King County: a firsthand understanding of what it takes to build a second chance into a real career — which is why KCD hires and trains crews the same way it wins contracts, for the long haul.

Today Kelly leads KCD Trucking alongside COO Jacque Julien, with a governance and compliance program built to hold up under WSDOT, Port of Seattle, and prime-GC scrutiny.

Built for Active Jobsites
