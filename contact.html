Before You Call

Frequently asked

Do you work directly with GCs, or only as a sub?

Both. KCD performs as a specialty subcontractor for prime general contractors and holds direct relationships — including active direct work at SEA-TAC Airport.

Can your crews access airside / AOA areas?

Yes. KCD runs AOA-badged crews at SEA-TAC and understands the access, safety, and FOD-control requirements of active airfield environments.

What counties do you operate in?

King, Pierce, and Snohomish Counties, Washington — the greater Seattle and Puget Sound region.

Do you carry prevailing wage / certified payroll?

Yes. KCD provides certified payroll on prevailing-wage jobs, along with the documentation support that keeps GCs audit-ready.

How fast can you mobilize a crew?

It depends on scope and schedule — call the dispatch line at (206) 909-0780 and we'll give you a straight answer for your project.
