A KCD Community Initiative

Pay It 4ward

Turning opportunity into careers — CDL training and trade-based education for the next generation in Seattle's Central District.

Our Mission

Opening the door to good careers, one trade at a time

Pay It 4ward is a Washington nonprofit founded by KCD Trucking's owner, Kelly Jefferson. It grew out of a simple belief: the same trades that built KCD can build stable, well-paying careers for people who might never get a first shot otherwise.

Through commercial driver's license (CDL) training and other trade-based education, Pay It 4ward helps people in Seattle's Central District gain the skills and credentials that lead to real, family-sustaining work.

What We Do

Practical training with a direct path to work

🚛

CDL Training

Helping community members earn their commercial driver's license — a credential that opens the door to dependable careers in trucking and logistics.

🛠️

Trade Education

Hands-on, trade-based learning that builds the practical skills employers in construction and transportation actually need.

🌱

Community Investment

Rooted in Seattle's Central District, Pay It 4ward invests directly in the neighborhood — creating opportunity where it's needed most.

Why It Matters

A hand up, not a handout

KCD has always believed that a job is more than a paycheck — it's stability, dignity and a future. That belief shapes how KCD hires and trains its own crews: for the long haul.

Pay It 4ward extends that belief beyond the company. By giving people the training and credentials to get started, it helps break cycles and build the next generation of skilled tradespeople.
