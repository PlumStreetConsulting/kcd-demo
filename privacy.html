Privacy Policy

How KCD Trucking Inc. handles information you share through this website.

Last updated: July 2026

What we collect

If you use the quote-request form on our contact page, we receive the information you choose to send us: your name, email address, phone number, company, and the details of your message. We do not ask for and do not want any sensitive personal information through this site.

How we use it

We use the information you submit for one purpose: to respond to your inquiry and, if you become a client, to communicate about your project. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Form processing

Form submissions may be delivered to us by a third-party form-processing service, which handles the data solely to transmit your message to us. If you prefer, you can always reach us directly by phone at (206) 909-0780 or by email at kellyj@kcdtruckingllc.com.

Cookies & analytics

This site does not set advertising cookies. If we add basic, privacy-respecting analytics in the future to understand site traffic, we will update this policy to say so.

External links

Pages on this site may link to external services such as Google Maps or the websites of agencies and partners. Those sites have their own privacy practices, which we do not control.

Your choices

You may ask us at any time what information we hold from your inquiries, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. Email kellyj@kcdtruckingllc.com and we will take care of it.
